Climbing & Via Ferrata in Ruhpolding

Vertical freedom in the Alps with routes from beginner-friendly crags to challenging multi-pitch climbs and secured via ferrata adventures

Alpine Climbing Paradise

The Chiemgau region around Ruhpolding offers some of Bavaria's finest climbing opportunities, with limestone cliffs, secure via ferrata routes, and alpine climbing adventures. From the famous Klettergarten Ruhpolding to the challenging walls of the surrounding peaks, climbers of all abilities will find their perfect vertical challenge.

Climbing Highlights

  • Over 50 sport climbing routes
  • 3 secured via ferrata routes
  • Difficulty grades from 3 to 8+ UIAA
  • Multi-pitch alpine routes
  • Family-friendly climbing garden
  • Professional guiding available

Safety is our top priority. All our climbing areas are regularly inspected and maintained. Our certified mountain guides provide instruction for beginners and challenging routes for experienced climbers. The combination of solid limestone, stunning views, and comprehensive safety measures makes Ruhpolding an ideal climbing destination.
