Mountain Biking in Ruhpolding

Experience the thrill of alpine single tracks, flowing forest trails, and technical descents through the stunning Bavarian countryside

Alpine Mountain Biking Paradise

Ruhpolding is a true paradise for mountain bikers of all skill levels. From gentle family-friendly trails winding through meadows and forests to extreme downhill tracks that challenge even professional riders, our region offers an unparalleled variety of terrain and experiences.

Biking Highlights

  • 150km of marked mountain bike trails
  • Flow trails, single tracks, and downhill courses
  • Bike parks with jumps and technical features
  • Panoramic alpine routes up to 1,800m elevation
  • All-mountain, cross-country, and enduro options
  • Professional bike rental and repair services

Our trail network is designed and maintained by experienced riders, ensuring optimal flow and safety. Whether you prefer cross-country endurance rides, technical all-mountain adventures, or gravity-fed downhill thrills, you'll find your perfect trail in Ruhpolding.
