Trail Running in Ruhpolding

Natural flow and alpine challenge through scenic forest paths and panoramic ridge lines in the heart of the Bavarian Alps

Discover Alpine Trail Running

Ruhpolding offers trail runners an incredible variety of terrain, from gentle forest paths perfect for beginners to challenging mountain trails that will test even experienced athletes. The combination of well-maintained trails, stunning Alpine scenery, and year-round accessibility makes it a premier destination for trail running enthusiasts.

Trail Highlights

  • Over 200km of marked trails
  • Elevation gains from 100m to 1,500m
  • Technical single tracks and flowing forest paths
  • Panoramic views of the Chiemgau Alps
  • Trail difficulty from beginner to expert
  • Year-round running conditions

Whether you're looking for a peaceful jog through ancient forests or an adrenaline-pumping mountain adventure, our trail network caters to every level of runner. The trails are well-marked and regularly maintained, ensuring both safety and enjoyment for all visitors.
