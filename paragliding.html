Paragliding in Ruhpolding

Soar above the stunning Bavarian Alps and experience the ultimate freedom with breathtaking panoramic views over the Chiemgau region.

Alpine Paragliding Paradise

Ruhpolding is one of Germany’s most scenic free-flight arenas. Steady thermals, generous launch meadows and a dramatic 360° alpine panorama make it perfect for both first-timers and seasoned pilots.

Paragliding Highlights

  • 3 launch sites up to 1,200 m ASL
  • Reliable thermals for flights > 1 h
  • Certified tandem & solo instructors
  • 360° vistas of lakes, peaks and forests
  • Full equipment rental & shuttle service
  • Flyable almost year-round (Mar – Nov best)

From gentle evening glass-offs perfect for photography to adrenaline-pumping thermic climbs, Ruhpolding has a launch window for every taste. After landing, enjoy Bavarian hospitality in traditional mountain huts scattered along the valley floor.
