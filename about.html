About ExploreRuhpolding

Your trusted guide to outdoor adventures in the Bavarian Alps

Our Story

ExploreRuhpolding was born from a passion for the outdoors and love for the stunning Bavarian Alps. Located in the heart of the Chiemgau region, Ruhpolding offers some of the most diverse and breathtaking outdoor experiences in Germany.

Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just beginning your outdoor journey, we believe everyone should have access to the incredible adventures that our region has to offer. From world-class biathlon facilities to pristine mountain trails, from challenging climbing routes to peaceful paragliding flights - Ruhpolding truly has something for every outdoor enthusiast.

What We Offer

  • Expert guidance for all skill levels
  • Local knowledge and insider tips
  • Equipment rental services
  • Guided tours and experiences
  • Year-round adventure opportunities

Our Mission

To make the incredible outdoor experiences of Ruhpolding accessible to everyone, while promoting sustainable tourism and respect for our natural environment.
