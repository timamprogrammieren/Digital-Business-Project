Winter Sports in Ruhpolding

Experience world-class winter sports in the heart of the Bavarian Alps. From Olympic biathlon venues to pristine cross-country trails, Ruhpolding offers unforgettable winter adventures for all skill levels.

Winter Wonderland Adventures

Ruhpolding transforms into a winter paradise from December to March, offering some of Europe's finest winter sports facilities. Home to the famous Biathlon World Cup venue, this Alpine destination combines world-class sporting infrastructure with breathtaking natural beauty.

Whether you're gliding through silent forests on cross-country skis, mastering the art of biathlon, or enjoying family-friendly alpine skiing, Ruhpolding's diverse winter landscape caters to every winter sports enthusiast.

Winter Sports Highlights

  • World-famous Biathlon World Cup venue
  • 80km of groomed cross-country trails
  • Family-friendly ski areas with modern lifts
  • Snow-sure conditions from December to March
  • Night skiing and floodlit trails
  • Professional ski and equipment rental
